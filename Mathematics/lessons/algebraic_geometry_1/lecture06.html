Definition 5 (Polynomial Map) Let $latex { X\subset \mathbb{A}^n, Y\subset \mathbb{A}^m }$ be affine algebraic sets. A map

$latex \displaystyle (F_1,\dots,F_m):X\rightarrow Y,p\rightarrow (F_1(p),\dots,F_m(p)),F_1,\dots,F_m\in k[x_1,\dots,x_n] $

is called a polynomial map. A surjective polynomial map whose inverse is also a polynomial map is an isomorphism.

    $latex \displaystyle (t,t^2):\mathbb{A}^2\rightarrow Y $
